About Me

I’m a web developer who recently graduated from General Assembly’s WDI course where I spent 12 weeks fully immersed in learning different skills such as JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, the MEAN Stack and much more. Not only did I learn the fundamentals of web development, more importantly, I learned how to teach myself new technologies and remain motivated while doing so.

Before GA, I spent my time acquiring a B.S. in Marketing and creatively expressing myself through dance. Just like in dance, the ability to create something out of nothing constantly challenges my creative side and makes me a better problem solver. My experience in marketing taught me to keep the user's wants and needs in mind and I plan to do that when I help build or maintain any future applications. Using what I've learned from my past experiences, I hope to continue growing towards front-end technologies as a Front-End Web Developer.
